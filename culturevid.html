Philippine Culture and Traditions

Filipino culture is characterized by a number of traits and values, including:
Bayanihan - A custom where community volunteers help a family, such as by carrying their house.

Hardworking - Filipinos are known for working hard to support their families financially.

Respectfulness - Filipinos are known for being respectful, especially in the workplace. They use terms like "po" and "opo" when speaking to elders or those in higher positions.

Great communicators - Filipinos are trained to be great communicators from a young age, learning to speak and write fluently in both Filipino and English.

Pakikisama - A trait that means "getting along with others".
