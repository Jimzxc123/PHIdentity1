DID YOU KNOW?



The Philippines is a popular tourist destination for backpackers and travellers due to its pristine beaches, crystal clear water and teeming marine life.
Whether you want to snorkel with whale sharks, sip coconuts on the beach or try your hand at some adventurous activities; this country has it all.
There is something for every type of traveller in this incredible country, and it isn’t hard to see why it’s a bucket list destination!
